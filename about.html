The process:

First you have to signup from the signup form.

Then you have to login form the same page by clicking on the login form and entering your registered password and username. After that you will be taken to the dashboard where your signup details will be displayed except your password.

To pay your toll online you must first add balance to your wallet and that can be done by using the inbuilt otps wallet.

After adding balance to your wallet you can select your toll plaza by name and proceed further.

You will be taken to the payment page where you have to select your vehicle type and journey type.

In the end a popup will occur with the transaction id and a message is sent to your mobile number.
